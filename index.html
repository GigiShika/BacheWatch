Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

This privacy policy applies to the BacheWatch app (hereby referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Giancarlo Pérez (hereby referred to as the "Service Provider") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address)
  • The pages and features of the Application that you use
  • The time and date of your use of the Application
  • The time spent using the Application
  • Your mobile device operating system

For the core functionality of the Application, users may voluntarily provide:

  • Reports of road hazards, potholes, accidents, or other incidents
  • Photographs uploaded by users
  • Geographic location associated with reports
  • Descriptions and comments submitted through the Application

The Application may collect approximate or precise location information when you choose to create a report, allowing the reported issue to be displayed accurately on the map.

Artificial Intelligence

The Application may use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies provided by Google Gemini to assist users by generating responses, recommendations, or informational content related to road safety and reported incidents.

Information submitted to AI-powered features may be processed by Google's services according to their applicable privacy policies and terms.

How Information Is Used

The Service Provider uses collected information to:

  • Display and manage reports on the map
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the Application
  • Detect misuse, spam, or fraudulent activity
  • Provide technical support
  • Enhance user experience and application features

Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Maps Platform
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Gemini API

These third-party providers have their own privacy policies regarding the handling of information.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Service Provider may disclose information:

  • As required by law
  • To comply with legal obligations or governmental requests
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the Service Provider
  • To trusted service providers who assist in operating the Application

User reports displayed on the map may be visible to other users of the Application. Personal information is not intentionally made public through these reports.
